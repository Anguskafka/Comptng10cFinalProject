This is a test passage used to test the working of the text editor.  Pressing the look up button would highlight the first occurrence of the word written on the line edit.  Selecting the word count button will prompt the textfield to display the word count of total and unique words, and selecting word statistics will prompt a display of words appeared in the passage from most frequent to least. To change the text on this field, type in the test.txt file that comes with the project.
